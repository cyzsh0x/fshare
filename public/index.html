User Guide

Getting Started

To begin boosting your Facebook post shares, follow these simple steps:

  1. Obtain your Facebook cookie JSON
  2. Enter the URL of the post you want to boost
  3. Set the desired amount of shares
  4. Configure the interval between shares
  5. Click "Start Boosting"

Getting Cookie

To get your cookie follow these steps:

  1. Download Webvium Browser
  2. Login to your Facebook account (dummy)
  3. Click the 3 dots on upper right corner > DevTools > Cookie Manager
  4. Long press > Select all > Copy
  5. Proceed to start a session in Fshare

Safety Tips & Reminder

  • Use at your own risk
  • Never share your cookies with anyone
  • Get post URL from fb lite
  • The post must be public
  • Use a separate account for boosting
  • Start with small amounts to test
  • Use reasonable intervals to avoid detection
